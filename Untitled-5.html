FAQs of Adam

Question 1: What is Adam?

Answer: Adam is a comprehensive social media marketing app designed to help businesses manage and optimize their social media presence effectively.

Question 2: How do I register and log in?

Answer: To register, provide your email and create a password. After registration, you can log in securely to access the platform.

Question 3: What is the onboarding process?

Answer: Upon initial login, you'll complete an onboarding form to specify your preferences and objectives, helping Adam tailor its features to your needs.

Question 4: How do I create a social media campaign?

Answer: Choose from available platforms like Facebook and Instagram, input your credentials for authentication, and define your target audience based on demographics or interests.

Question 5: How can I monitor and adjust my campaigns?

Answer: Monitor your campaign performance in real-time, make adjustments promptly for optimal results, and access detailed campaign statistics to make informed decisions.

Question 6: What are the admin responsibilities?

Answer: Admins oversee user onboarding, manage user accounts, review user-generated content for compliance, configure platform settings, generate reports, and ensure data protection.
